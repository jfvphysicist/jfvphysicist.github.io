Chapter 2: My First Talk

On Friday 9th, 2018, I will give my very first talk of more than 15 minutes. Indeed, as part of an initative launched my fellow master students, I will give a talk on the Hubble tension (link to their Facebook page). What will it be about, you ask ? Well, let me explain:
In 2016, a paper got published claiming there is something wrong with the expansion of the universe, more specifically, with how fast it expands. Since then cosmologists have tried to find how the problem can be solved. So during the presentation I will explain exactly what is the problem and then review with you what the solution could be.
You're interested but you think you're not smart enough, you say ? First of all, you ARE smart ! I mean, you are on this website so you probably are. And second, don't worry this talk will be as simple as possible, of course there will be a tiny bit of formulas but who doesn't like math, right ? So now that you are reassured, I can tell you where it will take place: Uppsala University, Ångströmlaboratoriet, Room 80101 at 17:30 Local Time.
If you really can't come, here are the slides I will use but beware that these slides do not represent all the talk's content, there will be a lot more.
Hope to see you there !
